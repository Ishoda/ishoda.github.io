GN-Connect – Digital Grama Niladhari System

Full-Stack MERN Web Application

Project Overview

Digital Grama Niladhari System (GN-Connect) is a full-stack MERN web application designed to digitalize the daily operations of Grama Niladhari offices in Sri Lanka.

The system provides a centralized platform for managing resident records, family details, document requests, welfare information, voter registration and administrative services.

The main objective of this project was to improve efficiency, reduce manual paperwork and provide a transparent digital workflow between citizens and government officers.

Business Problem

  • Manual Records - Paper-based record keeping caused duplicate data, slow updates and difficulty maintaining accurate information.
  • Slow Services - Citizens had to visit offices multiple times for certificates, requests and information updates.
  • No Central Database - Resident, family and administrative data were stored separately, making verification difficult.
  • Limited Transparency - Citizens had limited visibility into application status and service progress.

GN-Connect replaces traditional administrative processes with a centralized web-based system that allows citizens and officers to securely manage information and services.

The platform provides role-based access, digital records management, search functionality, automated reports and structured workflows to improve government service delivery.

MY CONTRIBUTION - Family & Residential Data Management

Module Responsibility

I was responsible for designing and developing the Family & Residential Data Management module of GN-Connect.
The module provides a centralized approach to register, manage and maintain household information while improving the efficiency of public administration processes.
I implemented CRUD operations, family registration, member management, relationship mapping, search functionality, PDF report generation and dashboard statistics using the MERN stack.

Household Registration - Create and manage household records with residential details.

Family Member Management - Manage family members and relationship information.

Residential Updates - Maintain addresses and household information.

Search & Filtering - Search families using NIC, Family ID and address.

PDF Reports - Generate printable family information reports.

Dashboard Statistics - Display demographic summaries and family statistics.

Backend

Authentication

Role-based access control provides secure access for citizens, GN officers, and administrators.

Challanges & Solutions

Managing Family Relationships

Maintaining relationships between family members required a structured database design.
Solution: Implemented separate family and member entities with relationship mapping.

Efficient Record Searching

GN officers required quick access to household information.
Solution: Implemented search and filtering using Family ID, NIC, and address.

Maintaining Data Accuracy

Residential information changes frequently due to migrations and household updates
Solution: Developed update workflows with validation and approval processes.

Digital Report Generation

Officers required official documentation without manual preparation.
Solution: Implemented automated PDF generation for family records.
