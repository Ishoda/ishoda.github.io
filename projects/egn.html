PROJECT OVERVIEW

Transforming Traditional Public Administration

Digital Grama Niladhari System (GN-Connect) is a full-stack MERN web application designed to digitalize the daily operations of Grama Niladhari offices in Sri Lanka.

The system provides a centralized platform for managing resident records, family details, document requests, welfare information, voter registration and administrative services.

The main objective of this project was to improve efficiency, reduce manual paperwork and provide a transparent digital workflow between citizens and government officers.

PROBLEM

Challenges in Traditional GN Office Operations

📂

Manual Records

Paper-based record keeping caused duplicate data, slow updates and difficulty maintaining accurate information.

⏳

Slow Services

Citizens had to visit offices multiple times for certificates, requests and information updates.

🗄️

No Central Database

Resident, family and administrative data were stored separately, making verification difficult.

⚠️

Limited Transparency

Citizens had limited visibility into application status and service progress.

SOLUTION

A Modern Digital Government Platform

GN-Connect

GN-Connect replaces traditional administrative processes with a centralized web-based system that allows citizens and officers to securely manage information and services.

The platform provides role-based access, digital records management, search functionality, automated reports and structured workflows to improve government service delivery.

DEVELOPMENT EXPERIENCE

Challenges & Solutions

Managing Family Relationships

Maintaining relationships between family members required a structured approach to represent household connections.

Solution: Implemented separate family and member models with relationship mapping.

Efficient Record Searching

GN officers needed a faster method to locate household information among many records.

Solution: Added searching and filtering based on Family ID, NIC and address.

Maintaining Data Accuracy

Resident information changes frequently due to migrations, births and updates.

Solution: Developed update workflows to maintain accurate residential records.

Digital Report Generation

Officers required official documentation without manually preparing reports.

Solution: Implemented automated PDF generation for family records.
